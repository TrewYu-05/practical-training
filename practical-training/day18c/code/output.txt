=== 全文总结 ===
人工智能是模拟人类智能的技术，广泛应用于多个领域，并随深度学习和大数据取得突破。

=== 英文翻译 ===
Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the intelligence exhibited by machines created by humans.
Typically, AI denotes the technology that uses conventional computer programs to emulate human intelligence.
The term also refers to the research into whether such intelligent systems can be realized and, if so, how they can be achieved.
Applications of AI include robotics, speech recognition, image recognition, natural language processing, and expert systems, among others.
With advances in deep learning and big data technologies, AI has achieved breakthrough progress in numerous fields, including healthcare, autonomous driving, and financial analysis.
